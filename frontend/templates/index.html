Cybersecurity

Defense in Depth

lock

AES-256 Encryption + RSA Signatures

Every field encrypted at rest with unique IVs. Doctors digitally sign records with RSA-2048 for non-repudiation.

link

Hash Chain Audit Integrity

Each audit log entry chains to the previous via SHA-256 hash. Tamper with one entry and the chain visibly breaks.

pest_control

Honeypot / Canary Records

Fake patient records that trigger critical alerts when accessed — catching authorized insiders acting maliciously.

fingerprint

Session Fingerprinting

Browser fingerprints hashed on login. Mismatch mid-session = cookie theft detected → auto session kill.

emergency

Break-the-Glass Protocol

Emergency access with justification required. Temporary 30-minute window, fully audited, admin-reviewed.

visibility_off

Field-Level Access Control

Not just who sees a record — but which fields. Nurses see treatment, not diagnosis. Lab techs see only lab results.
